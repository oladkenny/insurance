Insurance

WHAT IS INSURANCE?

Insurance is an arrangement of finanacial protection provided by a company against harm,loss of property or life. We have different parties that are involved in the process of insurance,some of them are;

  1. Insurer: These are the people that insure property or life
  2. Insured:These are those whose life and property are insured
  3. Premium:is the amount paid by the insured to the insurer

Services provided by insurance company

  1. It helps to minimize loss of equipment and factory
  2. it provides enough security for goods under shipment
  3. it serves as source of fund in case of health breakdown
  4. it provides continuous funds for those who have retired

Benefits of insurance

  1. Insurance enhances the commercial and economic development of the country
  2. insurance help the insured not to start life all over again but where the insured was before the loss
  3. insurance encourage saving habit to take care of the future
  4. insurance gives hope of compensation in the event of loss or damages to property
  5. Endowment insurance policy can be used as a means of taking care of oneself at old age

Types of insurance

  1. Vehicle insurance: Can be taken out by any person or business that owns a car
  2. Fire insurance: It's a coverage against fire hazard which may consume a business premises or personal property
  3. Marine insurance:Involves anything insurable concerning the sea. it's a compound name that covers insurance of any goods transported by sea and the vessle used for the carriage
  4. Life insurance: Everyone agrees that no amount of money can pay for the life of an individual. One obvious fact is that a lot of people die before they reach the age of fifty
  5. Pension insurance: Involves customer or employer saving part of their income which enable them collect what they have saved over a period of time after they retire from active employment
  6. Health insurance: It's a coverage paid by an individual against possible future,hospitalization. The National Health Insurance Scheme(NHIS) was introduced by the federal governmemnt of Nigeria,2003. An employer contributes a certain amount to be registered as the beneficiary
  7. Burglary insurance: It's the act of breaking into a business premises or a house with the intent to steal the owner's property
